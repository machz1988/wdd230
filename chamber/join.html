NP Membership

Free

$100

  • Privileges as private industry members of Chapter.

  • Research assistance for limited amount of service, with discount for additional service.

$500

  • Member discounts for all events.
  • Regional networking.
  • Special consideration for Trade Mission participation.
  • All other Bronze Membership benefits.

Gold Membership

$1000

  • Priority consideration for Trade Missions.
  • Priority access to Eraxamani Chamber staff assistance for research.
  • All other Silver Membership benefits.
